ABOUT THIS PORTAL

Understanding Our Customers, Better

At Rewaa, our customers are at the heart of everything we build. This portal consolidates three key feedback streams to help the product and success teams make data-driven decisions.

💡
Product Ideas
Customers submit and vote on feature requests, helping prioritize the roadmap based on real demand.
📊
NPS Measurement
Regular surveys capture loyalty trends, segment performance, and verbatim customer sentiment.
🎓
Training Quality
Post-training surveys track satisfaction per module, helping improve delivery and content quality.
